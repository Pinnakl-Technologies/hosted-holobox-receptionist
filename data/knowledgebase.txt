Ultimate Buildmart
1. Company Overview

Ultimate Buildmart is a Karachi-based supplier and distributor of premium interior and exterior architectural materials in Pakistan. The company specializes in high-quality finishing solutions for residential, commercial, corporate, retail, and healthcare projects.
Ultimate Buildmart operates as a one-stop solution provider, offering internationally recognized brands, technical consultation, and project-based material supply.

Industry: Architectural & Building Materials
Business Type: Importer, Distributor, Project Supplier
Primary Market: Pakistan
Head Office: DHA Phase VII, Karachi

4. Product Portfolio
4.1 High Pressure Laminates (HPL)
Brand Supplied:

Wilsonart
Formica
Arborite

Product Description:

High Pressure Laminate (HPL) is a decorative surface material made by compressing resin-impregnated kraft paper layers under high heat and pressure. It is widely used for furniture, cabinetry, and interior wall finishes.

Key Features:

Scratch resistant
Heat resistant
Moisture resistant
Impact resistant
Easy maintenance
Wide color and texture range

Applications:

Kitchen cabinets
Wardrobes
Office workstations
Retail counters
Wall paneling
Laboratory furniture

Hospital cabinetry
